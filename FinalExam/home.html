Welcome to the Oklahoma City Thunder fan page! The Thunder are a professional basketball team based in Oklahoma City and are part of the NBA's Western Conference Northwest Division.

Team History

The team was originally established as the Seattle SuperSonics in 1967 before relocating to Oklahoma City in 2008. Since the move, the Thunder have featured numerous All-Stars and made several playoff appearances.
